Welcome To My Guessing Game!

This is a very simple guessing game. After you start the game, I will think of a number between 1 and 100. I will give you 6 chances to guess the number. If you don't get the number correct, I will tell you whether the number is higher or lower than your guess. If you guess the number correctly in 6 or fewer guesses, you win! If not, I win! We will keep score on the scoreboard until you decide to quit the game!

First things first, though. My name is Gandalf. What is yours?
